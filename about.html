Biography

Vote for
Julio Guzman

Julio Guzman, a dedicated Homestead resident passionate about building a prosperous community. As Former Vice-Mayor and licensed Real Estate Broker, Julio works tirelessly to address job creation, attract new business, downtown revitalization, traffic issues, and addressing the city's permitting department.

A husband and father of three, Julio has called Homestead home for over four decades. Julio holds an FIU Master's degree in business and was a Carson Newman University dual sports athlete while being selected by the AZ Diamondbacks as a draft pick.

Julio's commitment to Homestead goes beyond politics. He is deeply involved in the community through civic organizations, business associations, and outreach efforts that strengthen the bonds between neighbors and build a more prosperous city for all.

Julio's Journey

Early Life

Homestead Roots

Born to Dominican parents, Julio has called Homestead home for over four decades. His deep roots in the community shaped his passion for public service and his commitment to making Homestead the best city it can be.

Athlete & Scholar

Carson Newman University

Julio attended Carson Newman University on a dual sports scholarship in football and baseball -- a testament to his dedication, discipline, and competitive drive that he brings to everything he does.

Professional Baseball

AZ Diamondbacks Draft Pick

Julio was selected by the Arizona Diamondbacks in the MLB Draft, achieving a lifelong dream and demonstrating the hard work and talent that define his character both on and off the field.

Education

FIU Master's Degree

Julio earned a Master's degree in business from Florida International University, equipping him with the financial acumen and strategic thinking needed to lead Homestead into a prosperous future.

Business

Licensed Real Estate Broker

As a licensed Real Estate Broker, Julio understands Homestead's housing market, economic development opportunities, and the needs of both residents and businesses looking to grow in our city.

Public Service

Former Vice-Mayor

Julio served as City Councilman and Vice-Mayor of Homestead, working to address traffic, jobs, permitting reform, and downtown revitalization. His record shows a leader who shows up and gets things done.
