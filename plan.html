A Vision for
Homestead's Future

Platform

Where Julio Stands

We the people achieve more than any single person could ever do alone. Here is Julio's comprehensive plan to move Homestead forward -- addressing the issues that matter most to our families, our businesses, and our community.

01

Traffic Management

Infrastructure

Homestead's rapidly increasing population has caused significant traffic issues in recent years. Residents spend too much time in gridlock instead of with their families. Julio has a comprehensive plan to address this head-on.

  • Partner with Miami-Dade County to fund key intersection improvements on Krome Avenue and US-1
  • Advocate for expanded public transit options connecting Homestead to metro Miami
  • Implement smart traffic signal technology to improve flow during peak hours
  • Work with FDOT to advance long-delayed road widening projects
  • Create dedicated turn lanes and pedestrian crossings in high-traffic areas
02

Housing Affordability

Housing

Previous development patterns have lowered housing affordability for Homestead residents. Too many longtime residents are being priced out of the city they love. Julio will advance policies that keep homeownership within reach.

  • Create a Homestead Workforce Housing Fund to support first-time homebuyers
  • Reform zoning to allow more diverse, affordable housing types
  • Crack down on predatory short-term rental practices driving up prices
  • Partner with developers committed to building affordable units
  • Protect existing affordable housing stock from demolition
03

Jobs & Economic Development

Economy

Emphasizing economic development stimulates growth and boosts the local economy. Julio will work to attract major employers, support existing businesses, and create pathways to good-paying jobs for Homestead residents.

  • Establish a Homestead Business Attraction Task Force to recruit new employers
  • Create tax incentives for businesses that hire locally
  • Support workforce training programs aligned with in-demand industries
  • Partner with FIU and Miami Dade College on job placement initiatives
  • Expand the South Dade Chamber's economic development initiatives
04

Downtown Revitalization

Downtown

Homestead can modernize while still maintaining its historic charm. Julio's plan will transform downtown into a vibrant destination for residents and visitors alike -- a place people want to live, work, shop, and gather.

  • Create a Downtown Homestead Master Plan with community input
  • Incentivize mixed-use development that activates street-level retail
  • Invest in streetscaping, lighting, and public art to beautify downtown
  • Attract signature dining and entertainment anchors
  • Expand the Homestead Main Street program
05

Permitting Reform

City Services

Homestead's permitting department has been a source of frustration for residents and business owners for too long. Julio will modernize the process so that projects move forward without unnecessary delays.

  • Digitize the permitting process with an online portal for submissions and tracking
  • Set clear response-time standards and hold the department accountable
  • Hire and train additional inspectors to reduce backlogs
  • Create a dedicated small business permitting concierge service
  • Conduct a full audit of the permitting department to identify inefficiencies
06

Public Safety

Safety

Every Homestead family deserves to feel safe in their neighborhood. Julio is committed to fully supporting our police and fire departments while building stronger relationships between law enforcement and the community.

  • Fully fund the Homestead Police and Fire departments
  • Launch a Community Policing Initiative to build trust neighborhood by neighborhood
  • Invest in mental health crisis response resources
  • Expand youth mentorship and after-school programs to address root causes
  • Upgrade public safety technology and equipment

Together, we can make Homestead the city we all deserve.
