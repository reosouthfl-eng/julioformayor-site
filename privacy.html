Your Privacy Matters

This Privacy Policy describes how the Julio Guzman for Mayor Campaign ("Campaign," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you visit our website at reosouthfl-eng.github.io/julioformayor-site or interact with us in connection with our campaign activities.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact information you provide voluntarily — such as your name, email address, phone number, and zip code — when you sign up to volunteer, contact us, or subscribe to campaign updates.
  • Contribution information — if you make a donation, we collect the information required by Florida campaign finance law, including your name, address, occupation, and employer.
  • Usage data — standard web analytics data such as pages visited, browser type, and referring URLs, collected through our hosting platform.

How We Use Your Information

  • To communicate campaign news, events, and updates
  • To coordinate volunteer activities
  • To process and report campaign contributions as required by law
  • To improve our website and outreach efforts

Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share information as required by Florida campaign finance disclosure laws, or with service providers who assist us in operating our campaign (e.g., email platforms, donation processors), subject to confidentiality obligations.

Florida Campaign Finance Disclosure

As required by Florida law, all campaign contributions are publicly reported to the Florida Division of Elections. This includes contributor names, addresses, occupations, employers, and contribution amounts. These reports are public record.

Cookies & Analytics

Our website may use cookies or similar technologies to understand how visitors use the site. You may disable cookies through your browser settings, though some features of the site may not function as intended.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

Your Choices

You may opt out of campaign communications at any time by contacting us at info@julioformayor.com or by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email we send.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
